## Boss: The Warden

When the 9th wave is cleared, [**The Warden**](/bosses/warden) enters the arena. Defeating it unlocks an **Ability Tier-Up** — evolve one of your owned tiered abilities to its next level.

<ModelViewer model="warden" />

### Mechanics

The Warden is a methodical guard who weaponizes the arena across 3 phases:
- **Phase 1**: Fires mortars (3 shots, 760 px/s speed) and bashes if you get too close.
- **Phase 2 (Zones)**: Creates prohibited zones (3 zones, shifting) that deal damage if you enter them.
- **Phase 3 (Ceiling Lock)**: Drops from the ceiling with a devastating lunge (1500 px/s speed).

> [!TIP]
> Use upward swings ("updrafts") to knock the Warden off-balance or parry its mortars back for massive damage.

## Lore

> The Warden's badge lies in pieces. Etched inside, worn almost smooth: "Appointed by the Council of First Light. Directive: none shall reach the Undercroft." Below it, scratched by hand: "I never asked what was down there."
