## Boss: Aldric (Berserker King)

When the 9th wave is cleared, [**Aldric**](/bosses/aldric) enters the arena. 

<ModelViewer model="aldric" />

### Mechanics

Aldric offers a fierce duel that evolves into a deadly desperation phase:
- **Base Stats**: 3400 HP, 130 px/s speed.
- **Throne of Fire**: Creates a checkerboard of fire that pulses every 3 seconds.
- **Fake Death & Frenzy**: Aldric will feign death and rapidly regenerate up to 50% HP (at 5% per second). During his Frenzy, he charges at 1550 px/s but takes 35% extra damage!

> [!WARNING]
> Do not let your guard down when Aldric falls. When he enters frenzy, prepare to dodge or execute perfect parries to counter his massive 1550 px/s charges!

## Lore

> Among Aldric's belongings, tucked inside the wrapping of his broken cleaver's handle: a small painted portrait, almost worn through. Two children, laughing. On the back, in handwriting read so many times the ink is barely there: "Elan and Mira — before the first Tear opened." And, in another hand: "Aldric. Come home." He never did.
