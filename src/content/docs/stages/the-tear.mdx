## Boss: The Source

When the 9th wave is cleared, [**The Source**](/bosses/source) enters the arena.

<ModelViewer model="the-source" />

### Mechanics

A floating rift that cycles every fallen boss's signature mechanics:
- **Base Stats**: 4200 HP, massive weight (7).
- **Mechanic Cycling**: Cycles a past boss mechanic every 2.5 seconds (Phase 1).
- **Floor Collapse**: In Phase 2, it rips platforms out of the arena every 1.3 seconds.
- **Fake Death**: Regenerates at 5% per second until erupting into its true form.

> [!CAUTION]
> Be prepared for the floor to literally disappear from under you in Phase 2. Use your dashes and up-drafts to maintain aerial momentum while platforms collapse!
