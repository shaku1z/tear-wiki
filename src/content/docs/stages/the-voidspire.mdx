## Boss: The Echo

When the 9th wave is cleared, [**The Echo**](/bosses/echo) enters the arena.

<ModelViewer model="the-echo" />

### Mechanics

The Echo is a dark reflection of your own silhouette:
- **Base Stats**: 3000 HP, high speed (280 px/s).
- **Mirroring**: Mirrors your tricks and attacks (0.55s copy delay).
- **Invisibility**: Cycles into invisibility for 3.5 seconds every 10 seconds.
- **Lunge**: A swift 1500 px/s lunge attack.

> [!TIP]
> The Echo mimics your aggressive style. Watch out for its sudden invisibility cycle and rely on audio/visual tells to counter its rapid lunges.
