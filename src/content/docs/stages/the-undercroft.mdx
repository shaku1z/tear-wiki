## Boss: The Iron Colossus

When the 9th wave is cleared, [**The Iron Colossus**](/bosses/colossus) enters the arena.

<ModelViewer model="iron-colossus" />

### Mechanics

The Iron Colossus is a tank with a front shield that forces you to maneuver. 
- **Base Stats**: 2900 HP, massive weight (9) preventing knockback.
- **Sweeping Arm**: Throws a sweeping arm across the arena (540 px/s).
- **Core Expose**: Temporarily exposes its core after heavy attacks.
- **Cross Panels**: Actively uses 4 panels for defensive shocks.

> [!TIP]
> The Colossus cannot be knocked back (0 knockback taken). Rely on mobility and dashing to get behind its front shield to deal full damage!

## Lore

> In the deepest wall of the Undercroft, beneath the mechanism that is now still, words are carved into the original stone — older than the machine: "Built to contain the Tide of Crimson. Should the Colossus fall, know this — we tried to stop it before it reached the Fields. We failed then too."
