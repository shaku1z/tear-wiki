The **Adventure** campaign unfolds across five distinct biomes, each with 9 waves of enemies followed by a boss encounter.
Enemy difficulty scales across stages: each new stage adds **+34% enemy HP**, **+14% enemy damage**, and **+2 more enemies per wave**.

## Stage Overview

| # | Stage | Blurb | Boss |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [The Grounds](/stages/the-grounds) | *Where order is kept.* | [The Warden](/bosses/warden) |
| 2 | [The Undercroft](/stages/the-undercroft) | *Gray industry, deep below.* | [The Iron Colossus](/bosses/colossus) |
| 3 | [The Crimson Fields](/stages/the-crimson-fields) | *Red and gold, and old rage.* | [Aldric (Berserker King)](/bosses/aldric) |
| 4 | [The Voidspire](/stages/the-voidspire) | *Where the rules thin out.* | [The Echo](/bosses/echo) |
| 5 | [The Tear](/stages/the-tear) | *Everything, all at once.* | [The Source](/bosses/source) |

## Wave Scaling (Campaign)

| Modifier | Per Stage | Per Wave Within Stage |
|---|---|---|
| Enemy HP | +34% | +6% |
| Enemy Damage | +14% | +2% |
| Enemy Count | +2 per wave | — |
| Concurrent Cap | +1 | — |

The concurrent cap never exceeds 10 enemies on screen at once.
