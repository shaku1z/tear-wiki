The **Adventure** campaign unfolds across five distinct biomes, each with 9 waves of enemies followed by a boss encounter.
Enemy difficulty scales significantly across stages, demanding absolute mastery of movement and blade-work by the late game.

## Stage Overview

| # | Stage | Blurb | Boss | Base HP Modifier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [The Grounds](/stages/the-grounds) | *Where order is kept.* | [The Warden](/bosses/warden) | Baseline |
| 2 | [The Undercroft](/stages/the-undercroft) | *Gray industry, deep below.* | [The Iron Colossus](/bosses/colossus) | +34% |
| 3 | [The Crimson Fields](/stages/the-crimson-fields) | *Red and gold, and old rage.* | [Aldric (Berserker King)](/bosses/aldric) | +68% |
| 4 | [The Voidspire](/stages/the-voidspire) | *Where the rules thin out.* | [The Echo](/bosses/echo) | +102% |
| 5 | [The Tear](/stages/the-tear) | *Everything, all at once.* | [The Source](/bosses/source) | +136% |

## Wave Scaling (Campaign)

Scaling occurs both *between* stages and *within* stages:

| Modifier | Per Stage | Per Wave Within Stage |
|---|---|---|
| **Enemy HP** | +34% | +6% |
| **Enemy Damage** | +14% | +2% |
| **Enemy Count** | +2 per wave | — |
| **Concurrent Cap** | +1 | — |

> [!NOTE] 
> The concurrent cap restricts how many enemies can be on-screen simultaneously. Although it increases by +1 per stage, it never exceeds a hard cap of 10 enemies at once.

## The Progression System

Clearing a boss wave at the end of a stage is highly rewarding:
1. **Ability Tier-Up**: You are granted the opportunity to evolve one of your owned tiered abilities to its next level.
2. **Score & Coins**: Modifiers applied by the current [Difficulty Tier](/modes) multiply your gains for meta-progression.
3. **Healing**: In the Normal difficulty, you receive 12 HP restored upon wave clear. (Sustain must otherwise be earned through skills like *Bloodrite* or *Vampiric Edge*).

Get ready to jump into the first stage: [The Grounds](/stages/the-grounds).
