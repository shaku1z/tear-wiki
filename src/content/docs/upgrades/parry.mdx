Parry upgrades turn your defense into overwhelming offense. Mastering parries (requiring a blade tip speed of **≥ 700 px/s** to intercept) is highly rewarded. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A successful parry already provides **0.18s** of slow-motion (`juice.parrySlowmo`) and negates all damage. These upgrades multiply that advantage.

### Projectile Deflection

<ModelViewer model="bomber" variant="lobber" />
*Bombers throw explosives with high arcs. Deflecting them normally applies a **1.25x speed boost**. With the **Reflect** upgrade, you turn a single hazard into a shotgun blast.*
