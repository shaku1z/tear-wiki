Mobility upgrades are essential for maintaining your `commitModifier` at 1.0x (which requires the player hilt to move at ≥ 620 px/s). They also enhance the dash, giving you more invincibility frames (0.15s) and repositioning tools.

> [!TIP]
> **Momentum** and **Slipstream** synergize incredibly well with the blade's spring physics. Moving faster naturally stretches the `maxReach` (120px) of the blade leash, resulting in violent snaps and massive `tipSpeed`!
