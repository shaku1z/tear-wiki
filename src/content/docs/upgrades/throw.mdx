Throw abilities transform your blade's projectile mechanics. By default, the throw deals massive damage (base 22 + speed bonuses up to **4600 px/s**) but leaves you vulnerable while the blade is in flight.

> [!WARNING]
> While the blade is thrown, you are entirely unarmed. If you miss, you must rely on your dash i-frames (0.15s) and movement speed until the blade auto-returns (after 2.5s) or you manually recall it.

### Recall Synergy

The blade's recall speed is **3400 px/s** (`blade.throw.returnSpeed`). It rips backward through everything in its path.
Combine **Storm Recall** with the natural 1.4x Throw Multiplier when at high HP to annihilate dense packs of enemies.

<ModelViewer model="ranged" variant="warlock" />
*Warlocks keep their distance (`preferredDist`: 380px) and fire curving projectiles. Pinning them with **Impale** stops their retreat and allows you to deal with them at your leisure.*
