Stackable upgrades are the most common draft picks. They have a base draft weight of **1.0** and can be picked multiple times in a run. Because these upgrades modify the physics engine's base variables directly, stacking them can produce exponential benefits.

> [!NOTE]
> For example, stacking **Fleet Foot** increases your `player.moveSpeed` (base 430 px/s), which directly increases the `commitModifier`, guaranteeing higher baseline damage for every swing.
