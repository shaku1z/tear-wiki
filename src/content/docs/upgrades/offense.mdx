Offense abilities dramatically shift how you deal damage, capitalizing on the physics engine's `tipSpeed` and introducing powerful area-of-effect tools.

> [!NOTE]
> All percentage damage bonuses (e.g. +25%) stack multiplicatively with the base damage limit of **58** (`blade.maxDamage`), the style meter multiplier (`trick.tiers`), and the commit multiplier.
