Tear's upgrade system allows you to build powerful, synergetic loadouts. After every wave, you are presented with **3 upgrade choices**. The draft pool is highly curated, weighing rarity against impact.

> [!NOTE]
> All numerical boosts apply directly to the base engine variables (like `blade.maxSpeed` or `player.moveSpeed`). 

---

## The Draft System

The draft algorithm weights upgrades based on their type:

| Type | Draft Weight | Description |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| **Stackable Upgrades** | **1.0** | Common numeric boosts (e.g., +Max HP, +Damage). Can be picked infinitely. |
| **Unique Abilities** | **0.6** | Mechanics-altering passives. Only appears once per run. |
| **Special Abilities** | **0.28** | Tiered mechanics. Highly impactful, defining your build. |
| **Rare Special** | **0.16** | The rarest abilities (e.g., Bloodrite). |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The game guarantees at least **one Special Ability** will appear in the draft per stage.

---

## Ability Tiers

Special abilities (those with a `tiers` property) start at **Tier 1**.
Each time you **defeat a Boss**, you earn an **Ability Tier-Up**. This allows you to permanently evolve one of your Special abilities to Tier 2, and eventually Tier 3.

---

## Upgrade Categories

Upgrades are divided into focused categories:

| Category | Focus | Synergies |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| **[Offense](/upgrades/offense)** | Raw damage, bleed/mark statuses. | Synergizes with `blade.maxDamage` and `tipSpeed`. |
| **[Resilience](/upgrades/resilience)** | HP (`player.hp`), shields, damage reduction. | Vital for aggressive trading. |
| **[Mobility](/upgrades/mobility)** | Dash cooldowns, `player.moveSpeed`. | Excellent for keeping the `commitModifier` at 1.0x. |
| **[Parry](/upgrades/parry)** | Projectile deflection (`deflectBoost`), slow-mo (`juice.parrySlowmo`). | High risk, massive reward. |
| **[Throw](/upgrades/throw)** | Projectile mechanics (`throwDamage`). | Synergizes with HP scaling and fast swings. |
| **Stackable** | Score, coins, raw stats. | See [Stackable Upgrades](/upgrades/stackable). |
