Resilience abilities in Tear are not passive safety nets. There are no automatic regeneration buttons—survival must be earned through skillful play, perfect parries, and aggressive mechanics.

> [!TIP]
> Your base HP is **100**, and your base jump speed is **920 px/s** (`player.jumpSpeed`). Many resilience upgrades reward utilizing aerial superiority to score kills and heal.
