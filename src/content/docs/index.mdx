## Welcome to the Tear Wiki

**Tear** is an unforgiving, skill-based action game where you wield a spring-physics blade against relentless waves of vivid, colour-soaked enemies. In a world where your foes possess all the colour, your survival depends entirely on kinetic precision.

This wiki is your survival manual: a deep breakdown of mechanics, raw stats, enemy behaviors, upgrade synergies, and optimal combat strategies.

## About Tear

> *"Skill is your only shield."*

Tear puts you in an arena against waves of enemies that escalate in speed, number, and ferocity. Between waves you draft upgrades from a rotating shop, slowly building a run that matches your playstyle. At the end of each stage a boss tests everything you've learned.

What sets Tear apart is its input fidelity. The blade responds to **how** you move it — the angle of attack, the speed of your wrist, whether you commit to a full swing or poke with the tip. Mastering that physical vocabulary is the entire game.

The [Style Meter](/mechanics/style-meter) rewards variety: chain slams, launches, parries, and throws together to push into **TEARING!** tier and unlock meaningful damage bonuses. Take a hit and lose half your meter — survivability is earned through reads and reactions, not passive regen.
