The Source cycles the previous bosses’ mechanics, removes safe platforms, attempts a regeneration sequence, and returns in true form. Its thresholds and cooldowns are presented in the synchronized dossier above.

## Strategy

- Read each inherited cast as a familiar boss tell, not a new system.
- Keep mobility options for the floor-collapse phase.
- Maintain pressure through the regeneration attempt, then reserve your cleanest defensive tools for true form.
