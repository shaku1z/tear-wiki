Aldric is an aggressive duel that escalates into a fire-pattern arena and then a false death. His live thresholds, recovery behavior, and frenzy modifiers are shown above from the game configuration.

## Strategy

- Learn the rhythm of the fire columns before extending a combo.
- Keep pressure on the downed state; stopping gives the regeneration sequence room to recover.
- Frenzy creates danger but also a decisive burst window when you can safely counter.
