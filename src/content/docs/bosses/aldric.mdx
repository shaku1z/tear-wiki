**Aldric, The Berserker King** is the Stage 3 boss. He begins as a fast, aggressive duelist, transforms the arena into a throne of fire, and ultimately attempts to trick you with a fake death before entering a lethal frenzy.

> [!WARNING]
> Aldric will fake his death at 20% HP (`fakeTier: 0.20`). During this downed state, he takes 70% less damage (`downedDmgTaken: 0.3`) and regenerates 5% of his HP every second, up to 50% max HP (`reviveFrac: 0.5`). You *must* DPS him while he is down to stop the regeneration!

### Phase 1: The Duel
Aldric matches your speed and aggression.
- **Fast Lunge:** With only a **0.45s windup**, he lunges at **1150 px/s**.
- **Shockwave Slam:** He slams the ground, sending out a **20 px radius shockwave** at **740 px/s** (dealing **18 damage**).
- **Relentless Pace:** His base attack cooldown is only **1.7s**, making him much faster than the Colossus or the Warden.

### Phase 2: The Throne of Fire (65% HP)
When reduced to **65% HP** (`fireTier: 0.65`), Aldric ignites the arena.
- **Checkerboard Flames:** He summons **8 columns of fire** (`fireCols: 8`) that pulse across the arena.
- **Pulse Cycle:** The fire pulses every **3.0 seconds**, creating a rhythmic jumping puzzle while you continue to duel him.
- **King's Charge:** Every **13 seconds**, he winds up for **0.5s** and charges across the burning arena at a blinding **1550 px/s**.

### Phase 3: The Fake Death & Frenzy (20% HP)
At **20% HP**, Aldric collapses, seemingly defeated.
- **The Regeneration:** While downed, he takes drastically reduced damage (`x0.3`) and regains HP at **5% per second**, capping out when he reaches **50% HP**.
- **The Frenzy:** Once he revives, he enters a Berserker Frenzy. He attacks wildly, but in his rage, he takes **35% bonus damage** (`frenzyDmgTaken: 1.35`) from all your attacks.

---

## Strategy Summary

- **Punish the Downed State:** When he fakes his death, do not stop attacking. Use your highest DPS combos (Power Slams and consecutive blade hits) to out-damage his 5% regen and kill him before he can revive.
- **Sync with the Fire:** The 3-second fire cycle becomes the metronome of the fight. Time your jumps and aerial combos to keep you off the floor exactly when the columns pulse.
- **Glass Cannon Frenzy:** If he successfully revives into his Frenzy, dodge his chaotic attacks and strike hard. His 35% damage vulnerability means a clean Perfect Parry or Power Slam will chunk his health massively.
