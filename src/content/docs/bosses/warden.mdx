## Phases

3 phases total. Phase transitions are triggered by HP thresholds.

### Phase 1 — The Patrol

Standard combat. Baton strikes with a 2.1s cooldown and 0.5s telegraph. Fires 3-round mortar bursts (760 px/s, 14 dmg). Bashes with knockback (620 px/s) at melee range.

:::tip[Strategy]
The mortar arc is predictable. Parry the first shot of a burst to get ahead of the rhythm.
:::

### Phase 2 — Prohibited Zones

3 danger zones (each 200 px wide) appear on the arena. Each zone shifts 7 times and ticks damage every 0.4s if you stand in it. Zone placement forces you to keep moving.

:::tip[Strategy]
Treat the zones like hot coals — glance at the floor, not the Warden, to track them.
:::

### Phase 3 — Ceiling Lock

The ceiling drops to y=150 px, severely limiting jump height. The Warden gains a lunging ceiling-dive (1500 px/s, 7.5s cooldown, 0.55s telegraph). Dodge perpendicular to the dive axis.

:::tip[Strategy]
The ceiling drop makes updrafts difficult. Switch to ground-based slams and throws.
:::

## Lore

> The Warden's badge lies in pieces. Etched inside, worn almost smooth: "Appointed by the Council of First Light. Directive: none shall reach the Undercroft." Below it, scratched by hand: "I never asked what was down there."
