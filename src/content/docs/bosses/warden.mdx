The Warden is a methodical arena controller. Mortars and baton pressure define the opening; shifting prohibited zones turn the floor into a positioning test; the final lockdown rewards baiting the lunge and punishing its recovery.

## Strategy

- Deflect mortars when the lane is safe.
- Treat the zone phase as a movement check: use air time and dashes to avoid being cornered.
- In lockdown, wait for the committed lunge, then spend burst damage during recovery.
