**The Warden** is the first true test of your mastery over the blade. Acting as the gatekeeper of The Grounds, he is a methodical, heavy-hitting bruiser who weaponizes the arena itself across three distinct phases. 

> [!TIP]
> The Warden uses base boss stats, meaning he is incredibly heavy (Weight: 6). You will not be able to launch or juggle him like a standard enemy. Focus on parries and high-damage Ground Slams.

### Phase 1: The Enforcer
The Warden relies on heavy melee strikes and artillery to keep you at bay.
- **Baton Bash:** If you stand within **150 px**, he winds up for **0.5s** before swinging his baton. This deals heavy damage and applies a massive **620 px/s knockback**, putting him on a **2.1s cooldown**.
- **Mortar Volley:** He fires **3 mortar shells** sequentially. These shells travel at **760 px/s** with a heavy arc (**900 gravity**). Each shell deals **14 damage**.
- **Shockwave Stomp:** Like the Armored brute, he can stomp the ground, sending out an **18 px radius shockwave** at **700 px/s**, dealing **18 damage**. Jump to avoid it.

### Phase 2: Zone Denial
Upon taking enough damage, The Warden activates the arena's security systems.
- **Prohibited Zones:** He summons **3 distinct laser zones**, each **200 px wide**.
- **Zone Shifting:** The zones shift around the arena at a rate of **7 px/s**.
- **Lethal Ticks:** Standing in a zone deals **7 damage** every **0.4s**. You must manage the boss while constantly repositioning to stay in the safe gaps.

### Phase 3: The Lockdown
In his final desperate phase, he brings the ceiling down.
- **Ceiling Collapse:** He locks onto your position, telegraphs, and drops a **150 px high** block from the ceiling every **1.6s**.
- **Desperation Lunge:** With a **0.55s windup**, he throws his entire weight forward in a devastating **1500 px/s lunge**. If you dodge this, he suffers a massive **7.5s recovery cooldown**, leaving him wide open for a brutal finisher.

---

## Strategy Summary

- **Bat the Mortars:** The Phase 1 mortars can be deflected. A Perfect Parry will send the 14-damage shell flying back into his face.
- **Stay Airborne in Phase 2:** The Prohibited Zones make the floor extremely dangerous. Use Updrafts and Aerial Raves to suspend yourself in the air, avoiding the shifting zones entirely.
- **Bait the Lunge:** In Phase 3, you want him to lunge. The 7.5s recovery window is your chance to dump all your burst damage (Power Slams and Throws).
