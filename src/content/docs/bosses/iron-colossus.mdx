The **Iron Colossus** is the unstoppable juggernaut of Stage 2. It is a literal tank, possessing 0 knockback susceptibility and a massive weight pool. You cannot move the Colossus; you can only survive it until you expose its vulnerable core.

> [!CAUTION]
> The Iron Colossus takes **0 knockback** from any source (`knockbackTaken: 0`). Do not try to push it or launch it. It dictates the spacing of the fight entirely.

### Phase 1: The Fortress
In its starting phase, the Colossus uses its immense size to dominate the ground.
- **Heavy Shockwave:** Winds up for **0.6s** before slamming the ground, unleashing a massive **24 px radius shockwave** at **720 px/s** (dealing **20 damage**). This is larger and faster than the Warden's shockwave.
- **Juggernaut Charge:** Winds up for **0.7s**, then barrels forward at an incredible **1350 px/s**. Because of its 152x150 size, you must double-jump or dash upward to clear it safely.
- **Base Attack Cooldown:** It attacks methodically, with a **2.5s** cooldown between standard moves.

### Phase 2: The Sweeping Arm
After taking damage, the Colossus deploys its sweeping laser arm.
- **Sweeper Laser:** It throws a sweeping arm that tracks across the arena at a height of **540 px**, moving at **540 px/s** and dealing **16 damage**. You must duck under it or dash through it.
- **Panel Armor:** The Colossus guards its core with **4 breakable panels**. You must destroy these panels sequentially to deal true damage to the boss.

### Phase 3: Exposed Core
Once the panels are stripped, the Colossus panics and floods the arena with crossfire.
- **Cross Lasers:** Fires intersecting beams at **640 px/s**, dealing **14 damage**. 
- **Laser Cooldown:** The cross lasers fire every **2.2s**, forcing you to manage tight dodges while trying to DPS the exposed core.

---

## Strategy Summary

- **Patience is Key:** With 2900 HP and armor panels, this is an endurance fight. Do not overcommit to long combos.
- **Verticality:** Its charge covers a massive horizontal area. Save your double jump or Updraft purely for dodging the 1350 px/s charge.
- **Break the Panels:** Focus all your heavy strikes (Power Slams, fully charged Throws) directly on the 4 armored panels. Once they break, the boss's health melts.
