The Colossus controls spacing through a heavy charge, shockwaves, a sweeping arm, and later crossfire. It cannot be meaningfully displaced; instead, create windows by avoiding its committed moves and breaking its armor.

## Strategy

- Save vertical movement for the charge and sweeper.
- Focus heavy attacks on exposed armor panels.
- Once the core is accessible, convert every safe gap into burst damage.
