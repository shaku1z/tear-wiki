## Phases

3 phases total. Phase transitions are triggered by HP thresholds.

### Phase 1 — The Mirror

The Echo copies your last trick with a 0.55s delay. It uses your own style against you — a slam will be followed by a mirrored slam. Fires projectiles (14 dmg, 820 px/s) and a shockwave (16 dmg).

:::tip[Strategy]
Switch tricks constantly. The Echo can't copy a trick it hasn't seen from you yet.
:::

### Phase 2 — Split

At ~50% HP, The Echo splits into two copies. Only one is real — the other mirrors your hits for zero damage. The real Echo is slightly brighter.

:::tip[Strategy]
Throw your blade at both. The real one reacts differently when hit — it staggers. The fake does not.
:::

### Phase 3 — Invisible

Cycles between visible (10s) and invisible (3.5s). During invisibility it still attacks — watch for shockwave particles to locate it. Lunge speed increases to 1500 px/s.

:::tip[Strategy]
During invisibility, keep moving and listen for audio cues. The shockwave particles give its position away.
:::

## Lore

> The Voidspire is quiet for the first time. On the wall behind where The Echo stood, scratched deep over what must have been years: your name. Over and over — hundreds of times. Below them all, fresher, still sharp: "I remember what it was like to be going somewhere." And at the very bottom: "Go finish it. One of us should."
