The Echo mirrors recent player behavior, splits into real and false targets, then attacks while hidden. Its live copy delay, visibility cycle, and attack parameters are sourced in the dossier above.

## Strategy

- Vary actions so the mirror has less predictable follow-up value.
- Use throws to identify the real target during the split.
- During invisibility, follow particles, sound, and shockwave tells rather than waiting passively.
