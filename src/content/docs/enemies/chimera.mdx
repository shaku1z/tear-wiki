The **Chimera** is a horrifying late-game amalgamation that possesses no original attacks of its own. Instead, it observes the other enemies currently spawned in the wave and adopts their movesets, seamlessly cycling between them.

> [!CAUTION]
> The Chimera will actively steal the mechanics of any enemy currently alive. If a Bomber and a Charger are alive, the Chimera might lob an explosive, then immediately bull-charge you.

### Mechanics

- **Mimicry:** The Chimera scans the wave. It can perform a Charger's dash, a Bomber's lob, a Ranged unit's shot, or an Armored unit's stomp, depending on what it observed.
- **Copy Delay:** After observing a move, it takes **0.55s** for the Chimera to adapt and execute it. 
- **The Windup Tell:** Because it can do anything, you must watch its animation windup to predict the incoming attack. The visual tell will mimic the original enemy (e.g., pulling back like a Ranged sniper, or glowing like an Executioner).

---

## Strategy Summary

- **Control the Wave:** The Chimera is only as dangerous as the enemies around it. If you kill all the dangerous variants (like Marksmen or Trappers), the Chimera loses access to their lethal moves.
- **Isolate It:** In a 1-on-1 scenario where it has no allies to copy from, the Chimera is forced back to a sluggish baseline melee attack.
- **Read the Animation:** Do not assume a Chimera is safe at mid-range. If it starts leaning back, a 1900 px/s Marksman shot might be coming. Treat it with extreme caution until you identify which move it has queued.
