The **Bomber** specializes in area denial. By lobbing heavy explosives that are affected by gravity, they force you to constantly reposition. The bombs can be deflected mid-air, making Bombers highly vulnerable to their own ordnance.

> [!TIP]
> A deflected bomb will fly back and explode on impact, instantly killing the low-HP Bomber (40 HP) and dealing **24 damage** in a **150 px radius** to any enemies nearby!

### Mechanics

- **Standoff Range:** Similar to Ranged enemies, the Bomber prefers to keep a distance of **340 px**.
- **Payload Arc:** Bombs are fired with an initial speed of **540 px/s**, an arc curve of **540 px/s**, and are pulled down by a heavy **1150 gravity** factor.
- **Explosion:** Upon hitting a surface, the bomb detonates, dealing **24 damage** within a **150 px radius**.
- **Lob Interval:** A standard Bomber throws one explosive every **2.4s**.

## Strategy Summary

- **Play Baseball:** Do not just run away from bombs. Swing your blade upward to launch them back. 
- **Clear the Board:** A Geomancer's wall can trap you in with a Charger or Executioner. Prioritize Geomancers immediately to prevent getting boxed in.
- **Mind the Mines:** You can safely trigger a Trapper's mine by throwing your blade at it from a distance.
