The **Flyer** entirely ignores ground friction and gravity, preferring to orbit above the battlefield before plunging in for a strike. They are exceptionally fragile and lightweight but demand you take your eyes off the ground.

> [!TIP]
> Their low weight (0.75) means that even glancing blade strikes will send them flying. Launching them into other enemies is a highly effective crowd-control technique.

## Base Attributes

<EnemyStats variant="flyer" />

### Mechanics

- **Hover:** Flyers prefer to float at a **Hover Y of 150 px** above the player.
- **Swoop Attack:** Every **3.3s**, they will target the player's position and perform a rapid swoop at **700 px/s**.
- **Aerial Independence:** They ignore standard platforms and floors unless knocked out of the sky by a heavy slam.

## Strategy Summary

- **Throw the Blade:** Since they only have 44 HP, a single thrown blade (base 22 damage + speed scaling) is often enough to instantly kill a Flyer, especially if you catch them during their descent.
- **Aerial Raves:** Flyers are perfect targets to extend your Aerial Rave style meter. Use an Updraft to meet them in the air and juggle them.
- **Watch the Shadows:** When a Highdiver is off-screen, watch for visual indicators or shadows on the ground to predict their dive timing.
