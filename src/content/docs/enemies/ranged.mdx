The **Ranged** class (often appearing as snipers, casters, or gunners) prefers to keep its distance, pressuring you with highly telegraphed but lethal projectiles. It forces you to either weave through cover, deflect shots back at them, or dive in aggressively.

> [!NOTE]
> The base projectile travels at **800 px/s**. You need a blade tip speed of at least **700 px/s** to successfully deflect it. A Perfect Parry (**2400 px/s**) will send the shot homing back at the attacker with bonus damage!

### Mechanics

- **Kiting:** It actively seeks a preferred distance of **380 px**. If you move closer than **250 px**, it will flee to maintain distance.
- **Fire Cycle:** It waits **2.3s** between shots while kiting. 
- **Windup:** Every shot has a **0.7s** telegraph before firing. The high projectile speed makes this telegraph your main cue to dodge or wind up a parry.

## Strategy Summary

- **Deflect and Conquer:** Ranged enemies are fragile (only 60 base HP). A single reflected shot is often enough to severely cripple or kill them.
- **Corner Them:** Their AI forces them to back up if you close within 250 px. Push them into a wall, and they will lose their mobility advantage.
- **Beware the Root:** Prioritize Chain Casters immediately in late waves. Being rooted removes your Dash and Jump, completely disabling your defensive kit.
