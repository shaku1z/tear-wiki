The **Charger** is the foundational melee unit of the Tear engine. It relentlessly closes distance to punish hesitant players, relying on its signature bull-charge to dictate positioning. While straightforward individually, in numbers or when paired with ranged support, Chargers can easily overwhelm you. 

> [!TIP]
> Wait for the 0.55s windup. The Charger cannot steer once committed to its 760 px/s dash. Dodge vertically, and if it slams into a wall, it will be stunned for 1.1s — a perfect window for a Power Slam.

### Mechanics

- **Bull-Charge**: At a range of **420 px**, the Charger stops and winds up for **0.55s**. It then bursts forward at **760 px/s** for **0.5s**.
- **Wall Stun**: If the Charger collides with geometry during its charge, it becomes stunned for **1.1s**.
- **Cooldown**: The Charger must wait **1.2s** before attempting another charge.

## Strategy Summary

- **Capitalize on Whiffs:** Chargers are vulnerable for over a second if they hit a wall. Lure them near edges or platforms.
- **Identify Variants Early:** A Gravedigger demands you get close; an Executioner demands you get airborne. Mix-ups between them are lethal.
- **Aerial Combat:** Since Chargers are ground-bound, using Updrafts to launch them neutralizes their threat entirely.
