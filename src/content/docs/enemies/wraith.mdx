The **Wraith** is an ethereal specter that hovers just above the ground. It is fundamentally immune to standard melee combat. Your blade will simply phase through its spectral form, dealing absolutely zero damage.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Wraith is immune to direct blade swings. The *only* way to kill a Wraith is to hit it with your **Thrown Blade** (Right-Click) or by bouncing a **Deflected Projectile** into it.

### Mechanics

- **Ethereal Body:** It takes **0 knockback** from any source and ignores standard melee hits.
- **Low Hover:** It floats **70 px** off the ground, allowing it to glide over hazards and small obstacles.
- **Relentless Pursuit:** Because it cannot be staggered by melee hits, it will continuously drift toward you at **170 px/s**, threatening you with its **12 contact damage**.

---

## Strategy Summary

- **The Quick Draw:** The moment you spot a Wraith, do not try to engage it in a combo. Step back, aim, and throw your blade. A single high-speed throw (base 22 damage + speed scaling) will usually execute it.
- **Ricochet Kills:** If a Ranged unit fires at you while a Wraith is approaching, parry the projectile. A deflected shot (`deflected: #1faf5a`) will instantly shatter the Wraith's ethereal form.
- **Do Not Get Cornered:** Wraiths apply immense spatial pressure because you cannot push them back with melee swings. If a Wraith corners you, you must Dash through it (using your 0.15s i-frames).
