The **Armored** is a massive, heavily shielded brute designed to act as a frontline wall. It possesses immense health, very low knockback susceptibility, and takes heavily reduced damage while planted firmly on the ground. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Armored unit takes **50% reduced damage** while on the ground (`groundDR: 0.5`). However, if you manage to launch it into the air, it takes **+15% bonus damage** (`airDR: 1.15`). You *must* use an Updraft launch to kill it efficiently!

### Defenses & Mechanics

- **Directional Shield:** It holds a front-facing shield (`#15c2c2`). You can break this shield by hitting it with an attack moving faster than **1500 px/s** (such as a fast blade throw or a heavy slam).
- **Enrage:** Once the shield breaks, the Armored brute becomes enraged, gaining aggression and attacking more frequently.
- **Heavyweight:** With a weight of **2.2** and only **3** knockback taken per damage point, you need a fully committed upward swing (Updraft) to overcome gravity and launch it.
- **Stomp Attack:** If you are within **400 px**, it will wind up for **0.55s** and slam the ground, creating a shockwave that travels at **560 px/s** with a **15 px radius** (dealing **16 damage**). It can do this every **3.2s**.

---

## Strategy Summary

- **Updraft is Mandatory:** Hitting an Armored on the ground is a waste of time. Perform a rising uppercut to launch it, then juggle it in the air where its armor plating cannot protect it.
- **Jump the Shockwave:** The 560 px/s shockwave travels along the floor. A simple short-hop will clear the 15 px radius easily.
- **Flank It:** If you cannot break the shield from the front, use your Dash to quickly reposition behind the Armored and strike its unprotected back before it can turn to face you.
