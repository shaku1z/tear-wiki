Achievements are permanent records of milestones across all your runs. Each achievement awards **Shards** — the meta currency used in the [Meta Shop](/progression/meta-shop). Achievements accumulate across all modes and runs.

---

## Rarity & Shard Rewards

| Rarity | Shards Awarded |
|--------|---------------|
| **Common** | 5 |
| **Uncommon** | 12 |
| **Rare** | 25 |
| **Epic** | 50 |
| **Legendary** | 100 |

## Survival

*Achievements for defensive and endurance play.*

| Achievement | Rarity | Condition | Shards |
|------------|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Spotless** | Common | Clear a wave without taking any damage | 5 |
| **Immortal Run** | Rare | Clear a full stage without taking any damage | 25 |
| **Marathon** | Uncommon | Survive for 10 minutes in a single run | 12 |
| **Iron Will** | Epic | Reach wave 5 in One-Hit mode | 50 |
| **Second Wind** | Uncommon | Survive a killing blow (requires a Revive upgrade) | 12 |

:::caution
**Immortal Run** requires perfect play across 9 waves + a boss with zero damage taken. Boss i-frames from non-lethal glances do NOT help here — any HP loss disqualifies the run. Strongly advised to attempt on Gentle difficulty.
:::

## Total Shard Potential

Completing every achievement awards a total of **1 397 Shards**. This is more than enough to unlock all permanent meta upgrades, but some legendary achievements (Wave 100, The Whole Pantheon) require significant investment to reach.
