## What is the Meta Shop?

The **Meta Shop** is a persistent upgrade store that sits outside of individual runs. Upgrades purchased here carry over across every run, in every mode — they are permanent enhancements to your baseline capabilities.

This is distinct from the **in-run shop** (between waves), which resets with each run. Meta shop upgrades are an investment in your overall player power, not a run-specific choice.

---

## Currency: Shards

Meta shop upgrades are purchased with **Shards**, which are earned through:

- [Achievements](/progression/achievements) — one-time shard awards per milestone.
- [Daily Challenges](/progression/daily-challenges) — 10–65 shards per day.

Shards do not expire and accumulate across all play sessions.

## Design Philosophy

The meta shop is designed to:

1. **Not invalidate skill.** No meta upgrade makes you invincible or trivialises any mode. The gap between a player with zero meta upgrades and a fully maxed player should be small enough that skill is still the dominant factor.
2. **Reward time invested.** Players who sink hours into Endless, Gauntlet, and daily challenges accumulate shards naturally. The meta shop gives those hours tangible long-term value.
3. **Provide meaningful choices.** Each upgrade targets a different aspect of play — mobility, damage, defensive options — so different player archetypes will invest differently.
