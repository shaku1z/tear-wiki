The **Meta Shop** is the heart of your persistent progression in Tear. Upgrades purchased here carry over across every run, in every mode — they are permanent enhancements to your baseline capabilities.

Unlike in-run drafting, Meta shop upgrades are an investment in your overall player power. The costs scale significantly per level, requiring deep mastery of [Achievements](/progression/achievements) and [Daily Challenges](/progression/daily-challenges) to acquire enough **Shards** to unlock the highest tiers.

---

## Currency: Shards

Meta shop upgrades are exclusively purchased with **Shards**, the persistent meta-currency. You can earn Shards through:
- **Achievements:** One-time shard awards per milestone (e.g., reaching Wave 100 in Endless).
- **Daily Challenges:** Rotating global challenges that award 10–65 shards per day based on difficulty.

Shards do not expire and accumulate across all play sessions.

## The Three Disciplines

The Meta Shop is divided into three thematic trees. You can invest Shards freely across them, but focusing on one discipline early can deeply define your playstyle.

## Design Philosophy

The meta shop is strictly balanced around three core principles:
1. **Not invalidate skill.** No meta upgrade makes you invincible. A maxed-out player can still die in seconds to a Boss if they fail their parries.
2. **Reward time invested.** The massive Shard costs of the final tiers are designed as prestige sinks for veteran players grinding Daily Challenges.
3. **Provide meaningful choices.** Do you want to be a glass cannon with maxed *Edge Alignment*, or a tank with maxed *Tough Hide* and *Aegis Plating*? The choice defines your optimal build path.
