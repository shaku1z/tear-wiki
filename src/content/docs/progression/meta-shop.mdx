The **Meta Shop** is the heart of your persistent progression in Tear. Upgrades purchased here carry over across every run, in every mode — they are permanent enhancements to your baseline capabilities.

Unlike in-run drafting, Meta shop upgrades are an investment in your overall player power. The costs scale significantly per level, requiring deep mastery of [Achievements](/progression/achievements) and [Daily Challenges](/progression/daily-challenges) to acquire enough **Coins** to unlock the highest tiers.

---

## Currency: Lifetime Coins

Meta shop upgrades are exclusively purchased with your **Lifetime Coins** (the meta-currency). You can earn these by playing the game — the better your runs, the more coins you bank.

Lifetime Coins do not expire and accumulate across all play sessions.

---

## The Four Disciplines

The Meta Shop is divided into four thematic categories: **Offense**, **Mobility**, **Resilience**, and **Utility**.

> **Economy Scaling:** Maxing out **Coin Magnet** early is highly recommended. More coins directly translate to buying more meta upgrades!

## Design Philosophy

The meta shop is strictly balanced around three core principles:
1. **Not invalidate skill.** No meta upgrade makes you invincible. A maxed-out player can still die in seconds to a Boss if they fail their parries.
2. **Reward time invested.** The massive Coin costs of the final tiers are designed as prestige sinks for veteran players.
3. **Provide meaningful choices.** Do you want to be a glass cannon with maxed *Sharpness*, or a tank with maxed *Toughness* and *Warding*? The choice defines your optimal build path.
