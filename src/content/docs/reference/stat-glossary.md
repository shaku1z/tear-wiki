## Combat

- **tipSpeed** — blade-tip velocity at impact; the primary melee damage driver.
- **commitModifier** — damage contribution from player movement.
- **angleModifier** — damage contribution from cut direction.
- **KnockbackTaken / Weight** — resistance to displacement and launches.

## Movement and defense

- **i-frames** — temporary damage immunity.
- **Coyote time / jump buffer** — input forgiveness around ledges and landings.
- **Deflect** — sends a projectile back through the arena.
- **Parry** — interrupts a timed melee threat and opens a counter window.

## Run systems

- **Wave** — one encounter in a run.
- **Stage** — a biome sequence ending in a boss.
- **Style** — the multiplier system built from varied combat actions.
- **Affix** — an enemy modifier applied on top of its family or variant.

Exact values are intentionally kept in [Mechanics](/mechanics/combat), [Modes](/modes/), and live entity dossiers so this glossary cannot drift.
