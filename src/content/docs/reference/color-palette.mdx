The arena communicates through colour: aggression, counterplay, player feedback, hazards, and support roles all have their own signal. This swatch board is generated directly from `CONFIG.colors`, so every entry follows the game source rather than a hand-maintained document.

<PaletteConsole />

## Reading the arena

- Warm signals generally announce immediate aggression or impact.
- Cool signals identify ranged pressure, movement, and high-precision counterplay.
- Player-facing effects form a consistent visual language across the blade, successful deflects, and perfect interactions.

Use the live dossiers for the gameplay meaning behind each enemy or effect colour.
