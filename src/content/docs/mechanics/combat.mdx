Combat in Tear revolves around velocity, positioning, and style. Every swing is evaluated based on the blade's physical simulation, determining exact damage output on the fly. 

> [!NOTE]
> All damage calculations are deterministic. There is no random critical hit chance; if you deal massive damage, it's because you swung properly.

---

## Melee Damage Formula

Damage is calculated continuously at the moment of impact. The engine evaluates:

```
damage = (tipSpeed − minHitSpeed) × damageScale × angleModifier × commitModifier × styleMod
```

| Variable | Config Value | Description |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| `tipSpeed` | up to **7000 px/s** | Speed of the blade tip at impact (`blade.maxSpeed`). |
| `minHitSpeed` | **950 px/s** | Minimum tip speed to register a hit (`blade.minHitSpeed`). |
| `damageScale` | **0.0092** | Multiplier for speed over the minimum (`blade.damageScale`). |
| `maxDamage` | **58** | Base damage cap before external multipliers (`blade.maxDamage`). |

> [!WARNING]
> Swings below **950 px/s** deal exactly **0 damage**. You cannot gently prod enemies to death; you must build momentum.

### Enemy Invincibility Frames (Hit Cooldown)

To prevent a single swing from registering infinite hits, enemies gain a brief invincibility frame after being hit.
- **Cooldown duration**: `0.18s` (`blade.enemyHitIframe`)
- This is **per-enemy**, meaning a single wide swing can hit multiple enemies simultaneously without penalty.

## The Commitment System

Tear penalizes "wrist-flicking"—rapidly twitching the cursor without moving the player character. The `commitModifier` scales damage based on the *hilt's* velocity (the player's movement).

| Hilt Speed | Modifier | Description |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| Stationary | **0.5x** | Minimal body movement (`skill.commitFloor`). |
| **620 px/s** | **1.0x** | Full commitment (`skill.commitRef`). |

> [!TIP]
> Dash into your swings or maintain high momentum while attacking to ensure your `commitModifier` remains at 1.0x.

---

## Poke vs. Cut (Angle Modifier)

The angle at which the blade connects with the enemy's hurtbox matters:

| Strike Type | Angle | Modifier |
|-------------|-------|----------|
| **Straight Poke** | 0° (Parallel) | **0.4x** (`skill.pokeFloor`) |
| **Clean Cut** | 90° (Perpendicular) | **1.0x** |

## Hitstop

To provide visceral feedback, impactful strikes trigger hitstop, briefly freezing the game state.

| Damage Threshold | Hitstop Duration |
|------------------|------------------|
| **> 22 damage** | **0.07s** (`hitStop.big`) |
| **≤ 22 damage** | **0.025s** (`hitStop.small`) |

---

## Thrown Blade Damage

Throwing the blade uses a unique formula, heavily rewarding player health and momentum.

```
throwDamage = 22 + (0.008 × launchSpeed)
```

The `launchSpeed` combines the base throw speed (**1900 px/s**) and the blade's tip speed at the moment of release (`throw.speedFromSwing` = **0.45**). The absolute maximum launch speed is **4600 px/s**.

### Risk vs. Reward: Health Multiplier

Your current HP modifies throw effectiveness:
- **Above 50% HP**: Throw deals **1.4x** damage (`throw.hiMult`).
- **Below 50% HP**: Throw deals **0.65x** damage (`throw.loMult`), but returning the blade becomes your best tool for survival.
