Combat is velocity, positioning, and style. The engine evaluates the blade at the exact moment it contacts a hurtbox—there are no random critical hits.

## Melee Damage

`damage = (tipSpeed − minHitSpeed) × damageScale × angleModifier × commitModifier × styleMod`

Slow blade contact does not register as a damaging hit. A single committed swing can still affect separate enemies at once because hit cooldown is tracked per entity.
