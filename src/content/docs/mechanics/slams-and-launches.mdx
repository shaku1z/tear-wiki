Tear's physics engine allows you to manipulate enemy positioning. Lightweight enemies can be launched into the air for juggles, and airborne enemies can be violently slammed back to the ground.

---

## Launches (Updrafts)

Striking an enemy with a fast, upward swing can launch them. This is primarily governed by the `launchMinUpSpeed` threshold.

| Variable | Config Value | Description |
|----------|--------------|-------------|
| `launchMinUpSpeed` | **1250 px/s** | Minimum vertical blade speed required to launch. |
| `launchPower` | **780 px/s** | The base upward velocity applied to the launched enemy. |
| `risingSpeedRef` | **850 px/s** | Reference speed for rising attacks. |
| `risingDmgBonus` | **+90%** | Damage bonus when striking upward (`0.9`). |
| `risingLaunchBonus`| **+70%** | Bonus launch power when attacking upward (`0.7`). |

*Flyers naturally hover around 150px. Updrafts are exceptionally good at catching them out of their swoop (700 px/s).*

## Slams

If an enemy is airborne (either via a launch, or naturally flying), a downward strike will execute a **Slam**.

| Type | Condition | Damage Multiplier |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Standard Slam** | Enemy in air, hit downward | **1.8x** (`blade.slamMultiplier`) |
| **Power Slam** | Downward tip speed ≥ **1700 px/s** | **1.8x** + **70% Bonus** |

### Power Slam Mechanics

To trigger a Power Slam:
1. The enemy must be airborne.
2. Your downward blade speed must exceed `slamPowerSpeed` (**1700 px/s**).
3. (Optional) The player can also be moving downward faster than `slamMinDownSpeed` (**1750 px/s**) for additional synergies.

A successful Power Slam grants `slamPowerBonus` (**0.7**), massively amplifying damage and driving the enemy into the ground with explosive force.

> [!TIP]
> Juggling enemies with Updrafts and finishing with a Power Slam yields massive Style points: **10 pts** for the Updraft, and **11 pts** for the Superslam (`trick.pts`).
