The Dash is your primary defensive and repositioning tool. Mastering the dash is essential for evading enemy attacks, managing spacing, and maintaining the `commitModifier` for your blade strikes.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The dash provides both high mobility and invincibility frames. Using it offensively to close gaps is just as important as using it to dodge.

---

## Dash Properties

The player dash is governed by strict physics parameters to ensure it feels responsive but requires commitment.

| Parameter | Config Value | Description |
|-----------|--------------|-------------|
| **Speed** | **1500 px/s** | Peak velocity during the dash (`dash.speed`). |
| **Duration** | **0.15s** | Total time the dash state is active (`dash.duration`). |
| **Cooldown** | **0.55s** | Time before you can dash again (`dash.cooldown`). |
| **I-Frames** | **0.15s** | Invincibility frames (`dash.iframe`), exactly matching the dash duration. |
| **Ghost Interval**| **0.028s** | The visual trail interval (`juice.dashGhostInterval`). |

---

## Momentum Retention

When a dash ends, you do not immediately stop. The engine applies an `endSpeedKeep` modifier, retaining a portion of the dash velocity and transitioning it back into normal movement physics.

- **Speed Retained**: **35%** (`dash.endSpeedKeep` = 0.35)
- This translates to roughly **525 px/s** of residual velocity (more than the base `moveSpeed` of **430 px/s**).

> [!TIP]
> Because you retain 35% of your dash speed, chaining a dash into a jump carries massive momentum.

---

## Steering

While dashing, your movement direction is largely locked, but you have slight steering capabilities to correct your trajectory.

- **Steer Factor**: **15** (`dash.steer`)
- This allows minor adjustments to bypass obstacles or curve around enemy attacks during the 0.15s window.
