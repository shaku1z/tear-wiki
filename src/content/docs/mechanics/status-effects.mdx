Status effects create sustained pressure and vulnerability windows. Their exact tuning is displayed below from the synchronized game configuration.

<StatusEffectReadout />

## Bleed

Bleed stacks. Reapplying it extends the pressure; allowing it to expire means rebuilding the stack count from the beginning.

## Burn

Burn is a short, high-output damage-over-time effect. It refreshes rather than increasing its magnitude.

## Mark

Mark increases damage received from every relevant source, including blade attacks, throws, statuses, and deflected projectiles.
