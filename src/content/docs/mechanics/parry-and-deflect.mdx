Defense in Tear is active. You can parry incoming melee hits with perfectly timed swings, or deflect enemy projectiles right back at them.

---

## Parrying (Melee Clash)

When an enemy attacks, swinging your blade so it connects with their hitbox during their attack animation triggers a **Parry**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> To parry, your blade speed must be at least **700 px/s** (`blade.deflectMinSpeed`). Slow prods will not register as a parry and you will take damage.

### The Parry Reward

Successfully parrying grants massive tactical advantages:
- **Total Damage Negation**: You take 0 damage.
- **Counter-Attack**: The parry deals damage to the attacker.
- **Stun**: The attacker is thrown off balance.
- **Style Points**: Grants **12 pts** to the Style Meter (`trick.pts.parry`), pushing you toward higher damage tiers.
- **Slowmo Effect**: The game slows down briefly for **0.18s** (`juice.parrySlowmo`) by a scale of **0.28x** (`juice.parrySlowScale`), giving you a massive reaction window to chain attacks.

## Deflecting (Projectiles)

Projectiles fired by enemies can be swatted out of the air.

### The Math of Deflection

When your blade hits a projectile, the projectile's ownership swaps, and it is fired away from the impact point.

| Variable | Config Value | Effect |
|----------|--------------|--------|
| `deflectMinSpeed` | **700 px/s** | Required blade speed to deflect. |
| `deflectBoost` | **1.25x** | Multiplies the projectile's base speed on return. |
| `deflectDmgMult`| **1.0x** | Base multiplier for the deflected projectile's damage. |

### Perfect Deflect

If your blade tip speed is exceedingly high (≥ **2400 px/s**, `blade.perfectSpeed`), you trigger a **Perfect Deflect**.
Perfect deflects often have upgrade synergies that turn them into screen-clearing explosions or chain-reactions.
