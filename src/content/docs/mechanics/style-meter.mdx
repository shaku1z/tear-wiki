Tear rewards aggressive, varied, and stylish combat. The Style Meter directly buffs your damage output as it fills, pushing you to combine different techniques rather than relying on a single move.

---

## Style Tiers

As you gain points, you ascend through the Style Tiers. Each tier provides a multiplicative damage bonus to all attacks.

| Points Required | Name | Damage Bonus | Config Base Multiplier |
|-----------------|------|--------------|------------------------|
| **0** | (None) | +0% | `1.0x` |
| **14** | **NICE** | **+6%** | `1.5x` (Base Logic) |
| **34** | **STYLISH** | **+12%** | `2.0x` |
| **64** | **BRUTAL** | **+18%** | `3.0x` |
| **110** | **SAVAGE** | **+24%** | `4.0x` |
| **175** | **TEARING!** | **+40%** | `5.0x` (Capped) |

> [!NOTE]
> The engine configures maximum raw style damage at `skill.styleDamageMax` = **0.40** (40% bonus).

---

## Earning Points

Actions in combat grant points (`trick.pts`). To maintain high tiers, you must continuously perform these actions.

| Action | Points | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Hit** | +2 | Standard melee strike. |
| **Throw Hit** | +4 | Hitting an enemy with a thrown blade. |
| **Deflect** | +5 | Deflecting a projectile. |
| **Launch** | +5 | Knocking an enemy into the air. |
| **Slam** | +8 | Hitting an airborne enemy downward. |
| **Updraft** | +10 | Hitting an enemy upward at high velocity. |
| **Superslam** | +11 | Performing a Power Slam (>1700 px/s). |
| **Parry** | +12 | Successfully parrying a melee attack. |

---

## Drain and Decay

The Style Meter is constantly draining. You cannot play passively.

- **Drain Rate**: **26** points per second (`trick.drainRate`).
- **Hit Loss**: **0.5x** multiplier applied to current style points when the player takes damage (`trick.hitLoss`).
- **Decay**: The decay scales up to **2.6** over time (`trick.decay`).

If you get hit, you instantly lose 50% of your current style points. Maintaining **TEARING!** requires flawless, varied execution.
