---
title: Playground & Tutorial
description: Training spaces for testing builds and practicing core systems.
---
Playground is the consequence-free test arena. Equip a build, choose targets, and investigate interactions before committing to a run.

Tutorial introduces the fundamental loop: swings, launches, slams, throws, recall, deflects, and parries. Use the [Builder](/builder) beside Playground to move from theorycrafting into direct practice.
